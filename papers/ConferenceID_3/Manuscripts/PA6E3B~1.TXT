Holden Carver is a double agent without a choice, forced into a role he never wanted. Left to fend for himself among the baddest of bad guys, he must evade capture by his former allies and avoid suspicion by those he is secretly gathering intelligence on. Now the snare is tightening and Carver must make hard choices. But just how much does evil mastermind TAO really know? And what will Holden do when given a chance to come clean? With his every move is a lie, will he even be able to see the truth?
