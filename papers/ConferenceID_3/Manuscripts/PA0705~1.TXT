KABUKI: METAMORPHOSIS collects the entire story for the first time ever! Complete with over 40 brand new pages of artwork, sketches, character designs, in- depth commentary on the layers of story, and an insightful introduction by Bill Sienkiewicz (Daredevil, Elektra Assasin). At nearly 300 pages this is the largest and most comprehensive KABUKI compilation ever. Also features a brand new dust jacket painting by David Mack, embossed cover, special end pages, gallery of original covers, and lots more! As the acclaimed writer on Daredevil (and now as the new DD artist!) multiple award nominated David Mack has matured into one of the premiere modern storytellers. New readers are searching for Mack's Kabuki work and THIS Kabuki story.
